How I help

Stabilizing and evolving your data platform

I help teams build and stabilize AWS + Snowflake + dbt data platforms so they behave like real production systems: predictable, observable, and easier to support.

Many engagements start with a focused audit & hardening sprint: understanding how things actually work today, identifying potential gaps, and agreeing on a short, prioritized list of improvements.

Sprint

Audit & Hardening Sprint

A short, time-boxed review of your current stack to understand what's running today and where the biggest risks are.

  • Review of current AWS / Snowflake / dbt / Airflow setup
  • Check for gaps in security, reliability, and cost
  • Clear summary of findings and trade-offs
  • Short, prioritized list of recommended changes

Build

End-to-End Pipeline Build

Design and implementation of AWS + Snowflake + dbt pipelines so data moves reliably from source to analytics-ready tables.

  • Architecture design for your AWS / warehouse stack
  • Ingestion, storage, and transformation patterns (dbt)
  • MWAA/Airflow orchestration and scheduling
  • Alerting, runbooks, and operating docs your team can follow

Support

Ongoing Ops & Enhancements

Lightweight ongoing help to keep your platform stable and steadily improving without hiring a full team.

  • Monitoring, alerting, and small reliability improvements
  • New sources, models, and data quality checks over time
  • Support for releases, schema changes, and refactors
  • Regular reviews so nothing quietly drifts off course

Working together

What working together looks like

  1. Discovery call. Short conversation to understand your stack, pain points, and constraints.
  2. Plan & proposal. A clear, written outline of scope, approach, timeline, and pricing.
  3. Implementation & handoff. Work delivered in small, reviewable chunks with code and docs.
