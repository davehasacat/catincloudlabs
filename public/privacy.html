Privacy Policy

Plain-English summary of what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Last updated: November 2025

What we collect

Only information you voluntarily send us - typically your name, email, and message when you email dave@catincloudlabs.com.

Server logs: Like most sites, our hosting/CDN may capture basic request metadata for security and operations, such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, and timestamps. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling.

How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide the services you request.

What we don't do

  • No advertising trackers or analytics cookies on this site.
  • No selling or renting your data. Ever.
  • No unnecessary data collection.

Retention

Inquiry emails are retained only as long as needed to serve you and maintain reasonable records - generally no longer than 12 months unless required for legal, accounting, or contractual reasons. Security/operational logs may be kept briefly for troubleshooting and abuse prevention.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information by emailing dave@catincloudlabs.com. We may verify requests via your email to prevent unauthorized changes. This policy applies to visitors of catincloudlabs.com and people who contact us by email.

Security

We operate with a security-first mindset (least privilege, private networking, encryption). For vulnerability reports, see our security.txt and our human-readable Security page.

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed to children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the “Last updated” date above.
