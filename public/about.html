About

Calm, reliable data platforms.

CatInCloud Labs is a one-person cloud and data engineering studio focused on real-world AWS + Snowflake + dbt stacks that are secure, understandable, and maintainable over time.

How I think about the work

I care about systems that actually work in production: clear architecture, predictable behavior, and a level of complexity that fits the team using it. No hype stacks or one-off science projects. Just well-designed platforms your team can explain and build on.

Private by default, built to be lived with

Most of my work starts with getting the foundations right: private networking, least-privilege IAM, KMS-encrypted storage, and observability that makes it obvious when something is off. From there, it's about steady improvements, not constant reinvention.

The stack I focus on

I specialize in AWS + Snowflake + dbt pipelines, often orchestrated with AWS MWAA (Airflow). That usually means: S3-based ingestion, Snowflake as the warehouse, dbt for transformations and tests, and Airflow for scheduling and coordination. All wired up with the same patterns I use in my own reference stack.
