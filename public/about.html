About CatInCloud

We believe in building things that work — no frills, no fluff — just well-designed systems that scale cleanly and stand the test of time.

Every platform we design starts with clarity and security: private networking, least-privilege IAM, KMS encryption, and observability as table stakes. We build what’s needed, build it right, and build it to last.

Our preferred stack for modern data work includes AWS MWAA (Airflow) for orchestration, Snowflake for warehousing, and dbt Cloud for transformations — all aligned to enterprise best practices and incremental, testable delivery.
