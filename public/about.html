About CatInCloud Labs

Enterprise-grade cloud & data platforms — built with care.

Our Approach

We design platforms with security, clarity, and maintainability front and center: private networking, least-privilege IAM, KMS encryption, and observability as table stakes.

Our preferred stack for modern data work: AWS MWAA (Airflow) for orchestration, Snowflake for warehousing, and dbt Cloud for transformations. We favor incremental, testable deployments over big-bang migrations.

Who We Work With

Teams that value reliability and correctness; startups leveling up for audits and scale; and data groups moving from ad-hoc scripts to repeatable pipelines.
