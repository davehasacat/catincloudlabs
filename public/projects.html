⚡ Automated Regime Detection: Late 2025 Snapshot

Beyond simply moving data, this pipeline quantifies market behavior using custom dbt logic. Here are three algorithmic signals detected in the Q3-Q4 2025 snapshot:

1. The "Mag 7" Trade is Fractured

  • Divergence: While the basket is often treated as a monolith, performance varied wildly. GOOGL rallied +74% with "Bullish (Confirmed)" signals on 59% of trading days. Meanwhile, META fell -10%, flagged repeatedly by the model for "Bearish Divergence" (rising price vs. falling flow).

2. Flight-to-Safety Validation

  • Bonds as Hedge: During the "Peak Fear" event on Oct 20 (Put/Call Ratio spiked to 1.79), TLT (Treasuries) showed a correlation of -0.59 against SPY. The "Macro Gravity" model correctly identified this as a risk-off regime where bonds act as a valid hedge.

3. The "YOLO" Index

  • Gamma Risk: A staggering 67.1% of top-tier options volume was concentrated in contracts expiring in less than 7 days. The single most active contract wasn't a hedge, but a speculative TSLA $500 Call (1.5M contracts), highlighting extreme structural fragility.
