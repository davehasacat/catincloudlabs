Projects

Enterprise Stock Market ELT

A production-grade data pipeline on AWS integrating MWAA (Airflow), Snowflake, and dbt Cloud. It uses private networking, SSE-KMS encryption, and fine-grained IAM to ensure a secure and auditable architecture for ingesting and transforming market data.

MWAA Private Environment

A hardened Airflow deployment using AWS MWAA within a fully private VPC. It connects to ECR, CloudWatch, KMS, and Secrets Manager through VPC endpoints to eliminate public traffic while maintaining observability and control.

Future: Dashboards & Apps

Planned development includes interactive dashboards using Plotly Dash and Streamlit, hosted on AWS App Runner or ECS and distributed globally via Cloudflare. These apps will extend the ELT pipeline into live analytics experiences.
